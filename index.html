About Annai Hospital

Healing Hands,
Trusted Care

Located inside the SKSS Arts College campus in Thiruppanandal, Annai Hospital has been a pillar of healthcare for the community — offering round-the-clock medical services with warmth, skill, and dedication.

Our team of specialist doctors and trained staff are committed to providing each patient with personalized, evidence-based care in a safe and comfortable environment.

24/7 Round-the-Clock Care

Fully operational day and night — our emergency team and on-call doctors are always ready.

Advanced Medical Equipment

Modern diagnostic tools and surgical equipment to ensure accurate, timely treatment.
